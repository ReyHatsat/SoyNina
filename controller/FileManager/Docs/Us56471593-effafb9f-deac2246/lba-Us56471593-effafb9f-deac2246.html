2. Loan Amount and Purpose

The loan amount equals to the amount specified under Section 1 under “loan amount”. Borrower will use the funds to fund the construction of the El Lugar Resort. The Borrower is the owner of the land and the project.



3. Term & Effective Date:


  • 3.1 Term:

    The loan starts with the purchase of the loan bond and ends with the last payment as specified under Section 1.

  • 3.2 Effective Date:

    This loan agreement is completed and effective as soon as the loan amount has been paid online using the check-out online payment. In case of a bank transfer, the loan agreement is effective the day the funds arrive at Borrowers bank account.



4. Repayment:

The loan shall be paid back as indicated in the pay-out dates under Section 1.



5. High Risk Investment:

This loan is a subordinated loan and will rank after the bank financing of the Borrower. In case of economic difficulties, the bank loan will be paid back first and the loan bond payments will be delayed. In case of bankruptcy of the Borrower, the investment is totally lost. The Lender is therefore advised to only invest funds that are currently not needed; The question to ask yourself is: Will I be in trouble if I totally loose the money invested? If the answer is yes, please do not lend us money! This investment should be light and fun, and not a burden.



6. Cancellation / Transfer:


  • 6.1 Cancellation:

    If you want to cancel your investment 50% of the capital will be paid back within 30 days of receiving your cancellation notice, the remaining 50% will be paid back over a period of 3 to 5 years as the cash liquidity of the project allows.

  • 6.2 Transfer:

    A transfer to someone else or selling the loan bond is only possible if the new Lender submits all required documents and also becomes a member of the El Lugar Club. A transaction fee of USD 150 is due to the Borrower to cover the extra costs.
